Yep, it's true. Simply look below image and.. think.

Now ask yourself the reason.. where is the fault? In Facebook or in people that use it?

Facebook is a great tool, a great social network, something incredible. You can use it un several ways: for your business, to enjoy with games, to chat with away friends, look their photos, and many many ways.

But what happens if you use Facebook in wrong way? I can't answer for you, but you can do it. Think how Facebook can improve your life, think about benefits it can give you and think how you can use it.

Facebook is powerful. Don't let it ruins you.
